Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Who We Are

L7R Character Builder is a small, invitation-only web application run by the game master of a tabletop RPG group. All users are personally known to each other in real life. This is not a commercial service.

What We Collect

When you log in with Discord, we receive and store:

  • Your Discord user ID (a numeric identifier)
  • Your Discord display name (username)

We request only the identify scope from Discord. We do not access your email, friend list, server memberships, messages, or any other Discord data.

We also store any character data you create within the application (names, stats, notes, etc.).

What Is Public

All character sheets are publicly viewable without requiring a login. Anyone with the URL can see character names, stats, notes, and other sheet data. Do not put sensitive personal information in your character sheets.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not send emails or notifications

Data Storage

Your data is stored in a SQLite database on a server hosted by Fly.io in the United States. Session data is stored server-side; we use a secure cookie containing only a session identifier.

Data Deletion

You can delete any character you created at any time. If you'd like your account data removed entirely, ask the GM and we'll take care of it.

Changes

This policy may be updated occasionally. Since this is an invitation-only application for a group of friends, we'll communicate any meaningful changes directly.

Contact

If you have questions, talk to the GM. You know who they are.
