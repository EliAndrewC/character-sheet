Rokugani currency

Rokugan is on the "rice standard": 1 koku is a gold coin, and is always legally equal to 40 gallons of rice - the amount needed to feed one person for a year - for rent, taxes, and stipends.

1 koku gold coin = 10 bu / 100 zeni / 1000 sen
1 bu silver coin = 10 zeni / 100 sen
1 zeni copper coin = 10 sen
1 sen small copper smallest unit

Sen are minted as a connected line of 10 that can be severed to make change.
