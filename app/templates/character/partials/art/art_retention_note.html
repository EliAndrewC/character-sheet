{# Reusable note shown when a character already has saved art, on both the art landing page and the generate wizard. Explains that previous art is recoverable. No em-dashes per project style. #}

Your current art is safe.

Saving new art replaces what's shown now, but it does not erase your old art. There are two ways to keep an earlier version:

  • Save a copy yourself: open the current image and download it. You can re-upload any image later.
  • The last several versions are kept automatically. From this character's art page, you or the GM can restore an earlier saved version at any time.
