## Background

Tomoe was born to the Kakita family and raised in the courts of the Crane
clan. Her iaijutsu form is quick and precise, though her tendency to
contradict her peers has made her a difficult presence at formal events.
Despite this, her charm and kind eyes have earned her the respect of her
sensei.

## Appearance

Tall for a Crane, with pale silver hair kept in a long braid. Wears the
muted colors of the Kakita Dueling Academy and carries a katana forged by
her grandmother.
