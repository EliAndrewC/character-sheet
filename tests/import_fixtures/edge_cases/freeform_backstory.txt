Honor 3.0, Rank 7.5, Recognition 7.5

Backstory

Kenshiro was the third son of a minor Daidoji vassal family, born during
a bitter winter in the mountains near the Crab border. His mother, a
Crane courtier, had been sent to the family as a peace offering after
a long dispute over water rights. When he was six, his father died
defending a caravan from bandits, and his mother returned Kenshiro and
his brothers to the Daidoji family proper to be raised in the dueling
academy.

He excelled at the Daidoji style of combat - preferring counterattack
and patience over aggression - but his quick temper nearly got him
expelled twice. His third-year sensei, Daidoji Uji, saw potential in
him and took him on as a personal student. Uji taught him that the
best sword is the one that is never drawn, a lesson that Kenshiro is
still trying to live by.

Physical Description

Tall and lean, with black hair kept in a tight topknot and a short,
sharp beard. Silver-grey eyes. Wears the blue and white of the Crane
but with a conspicuous grey sash marking his family's shame over the
water-rights incident.

Goals

To restore his family's honor, ideally through a public duel against
a member of the rival family. To find out what really happened to his
father the night he died.
