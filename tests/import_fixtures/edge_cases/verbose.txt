The Chronicle of Isawa Kaede
a character ledger
maintained by her player

There is a long history to Isawa Kaede, and I am tired of summarising it
when people ask. Let me put it all in one place.

Kaede was born in the Isawa lands shortly after the Scorpion Clan Coup.
Her family had served the Isawa for four generations as phoenix keepers,
and she herself entered the Isawa Shugenja school at the age of ten. She
excelled at void magic from the first, though her teachers worried that
she studied the void ring at the expense of all others.

(The GM asked me for "actual stats" so here they are buried in the middle
of all of this prose. I'm not rewriting the document.)

She carries the following rings: Air is a 2. Fire is also just a 2.
Earth is a 2 as well. Water is a 2. Void, her gift, is a 4.

Her school is the Isawa Ishi.

She took the following advantages: Higher Purpose, her purpose being
to protect the veil between worlds. Disadvantage: she is Humble - she
downplays her own gifts, to the frustration of her sensei.

Her school knacks are the usual Isawa Ishi knacks: Absorb Void at
rank 2, Kharmic Spin at rank 2, and Otherworldliness at rank 3.

She has one skill, Precepts, at rank 2. She has no other skills, which
her school has reminded her of often.

Her honor is a full 4.0 (the Isawa respect honor even in their quietest
students). Rank 7.5. Recognition 7.5.

She has a knack for making friends with spirits. A fox spirit visits
her in dreams and has been teaching her to hear the voices of things
that should not speak. She has not told her sensei about this.

She is small and pale, with black hair and green eyes, and she is
almost never seen without her carved wooden prayer beads.
